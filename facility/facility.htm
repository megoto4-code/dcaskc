Facility

All of the computer systems (IBM PC) in the department are interconnected by a switched Ethernet local network, which is connected to the Internet via  the campus backbone. The department has two main servers, one of which is Windows server 2003 based, and one windows server 2008 based.  For the students, there are two well equipped, centrally air conditioned laboratories in the department. The "Pankaj Kumar Deb Memorial Laboratory" has approximate 30 PCs. and the "Central Computer Laboratory" has PCs with LCD monitors. The machines are a mix of P4 and Pentium Dual Core. The department also has one projector.

VPN Broadband Connection (under National Mission for Education (nme), Govt. of India) is provided by BSNL for Unlimited Internet access to the Departmental Students.

One Dot Matrix Printer and one laser printer are available for the printing needs of the students.  The department has a collection of softwares as per the demand of the courses and interest of the students.
